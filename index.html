About Us

Swift Rev Motors is the brainchild of two individuals brought together by a shared love for customizing cars and an unwavering bond. As co-workers and automotive enthusiasts, Danfil Lapena and Austin Johnson decided to turn their passion into a mission, to help others realize their dream cars. The journey began when Dan and Austin met at a local car show and discovered their shared fascination with unique customizations. What started as casual conversations evolved into an inspired vision. They understood that there were countless others who shared their dreams of driving personalized, one-of-a-kind vehicles. From these conversations, Swift Rev Moters emerged. Our mission is clear: we're here to empower individuals and fellow car aficionados to bring their dream cars to life. We know that a car isn't just a machine; it's a reflection of your identity and a canvas for self-expression. With this in mind, we've created a platform where anyone can make their vehicle a rolling masterpiece, just as we envisioned. Our journey is defined by dedication, hard work, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. We've assembled a team of artisans, designers, and technicians who share our passion and have the expertise to transform dreams into reality. Using top-tier materials and cutting-edge techniques, we ensure that each project is a testament to our customer's unique style. At Swift Rev Motors, we don't just customize cars; we fulfill dreams on wheels. Whether it's a striking paint job, heart-pounding performance upgrades, or distinctive accessories, we're here to offer the know-how and support you need. From our chance encounter to the countless smiles we've witnessed on our customers' faces, our journey is marked by a shared passion, tireless effort, and a relentless pursuit of perfection. Join us on this exciting adventure, and together, we'll make your dream car a reality. At Swift Rev Motors, we're not just a business; we're a community of automotive enthusiasts who believe the road to your dream car should be as thrilling as the car itself.
